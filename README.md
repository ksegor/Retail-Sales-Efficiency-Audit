# Retail Sales & Operations Efficiency Audit

## [EN] Project Overview
Comprehensive analysis of retail sales data to identify top-performing sellers, profitable products, and customer demographics. The goal was to optimize sales strategy and improve operational efficiency.

### üöÄ Key Achievements:
* **Seller Performance:** Identified top 10 sellers contributing the bulk of revenue and isolated low-performing segments for training.
* **Product Insights:** Ranked products by both quantity and profitability to adjust inventory levels.
* **Customer Demographics:** Segmented customers by age, revealing that **60.5%** of the client base belongs to the **40+** category.
* **Seasonality & Trends:** Analyzed monthly acquisition trends and daily income patterns to optimize staffing.

**Stack:** PostgreSQL, Python (Matplotlib/Seaborn).
